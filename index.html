WELCOME TO BANGALORE TRAVEL BLOG

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" because of its role as the nation's leading IT exporter. Apart from being a major IT hub, Bangalore is also known for its pleasant climate, beautiful parks, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage. .

know about some famous places before your travel

featured places

Lalbagh Road,South Bangalore

This beautiful garden is spread over 240 acres and is home of plant species, including some rare ones. You can take a leisurely walk, relax by the lake or even have a picnic with family and friends.

Cubbon Park - Kasturba Road, Central Bangalore

Cubbon Park is a sprawling public park located in the heart of Bangalore city. It is an ideal place for nature lovers, joggers and walkers. It is also home to several government buildings,including the State Central Library, the Attara Kacheri (High Court) and the Vidhana Soudha.

Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace - Albert Victor Road, Chamrajpet, South Bangalore

This palace was built by Tipu Sultan, a ruler of the Kingdom of Mysore, in the 18th century. The palace is made entirely of teak wood and has beautiful balconies, arches and pillars. It is now a museum and houses artifacts from Tipu Sultan's era.

Nandi Hills - Chikkaballapur district, North Bangalore

Nandi Hills is a popular hill station located about 60 km from Bangalore.It is a great place to escape the hustle and bustleCity and enjoy the scenic beauty.You can watch the sunrise,or visit the ancient Nandi Temple.

Wonderla Amusement Park - Mysore Road, West Bangalore

. It is a popular destination for thrill-seekers and families alike, with over 60 rides and attractions, including roller coasters, water rides, and thrill rides.

ISKCON Temple - Hare Krishna Hill, West Bangalore

Yes, the ISKCON Temple is located on Hare Krishna Hill in West Bangalore. It is a magnificent temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, and is one of the largest ISKCON temples in the world.
