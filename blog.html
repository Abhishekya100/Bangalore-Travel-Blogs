Foods in Bangalore

April 19, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Dosa

A popular breakfast item in Bangalore, dosa is a thin crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is served with different types of chutneys and sambar.

April 24, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Idli

Another popular breakfast item, idli is a steamed cake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is served with chutney and sambar..

April 25, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Vada

A deep-fried savory snack made from lentil batter, vada is a popular street food in Bangalore. It is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside and is served with chutney and sambar.

April 25, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Bisi Bele Bath

A flavorful rice dish made with lentils, vegetables, and spices, Bisi Bele Bath is a popular lunch item in Bangalore.

April 28, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Ragi Mudde:

A staple food in rural Karnataka, Ragi Mudde is a ball-shaped dish made from finger millet flour. It is typically eaten with sambar or a spicy curry.

April 27, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Mysore Pak

A popular sweet dish from the city of Mysore, Mysore Pak is made from chickpea flour, ghee, and sugar. It has a crumbly texture and a rich flavor.

April 27, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Filter Coffee

A popular beverage in South India, filter coffee is made by passing hot water through a metal filter containing finely ground coffee powder. It is typically served with milk and sugar.

April 28, 2023
travel | lokeshwaran Akki Roti

Akki Roti is a famous breakfast dish in Bangalore that is made from rice flour. The flour is mixed with grated coconut, finely chopped onions, coriander leaves, green chilies, and salt to make a dough.
